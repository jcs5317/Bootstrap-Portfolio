About Me

I have been fortunate to live, learn, and work most of my life in beautiful Arizona. Beginning in 1990 when my family and I first step foot in the state, there have been many instances that were meaningful and have helped me become who I am today.

Every letter of the alphabet has a positive acronym that describes myself and the connections I have with this amazing place and people here. Amiable truly is a quality adjective I find necessary to begin with, it is so important to me that I constantly remind myself that being friendly should be like a reflex and the more amicable I become will help those people around me be so too. Astonishingly there are a few words that begin with “z,” like “zippy” that I embrace as well. If I am demonstrating at least a hint of zippiness each day I feel there is a good chance at a zestful life.
